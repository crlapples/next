Chinese 5000

Chinese 5000 is an innovative app designed to help users master 5,400 Chinese characters across 52 learning cards. Each card presents 106 characters, arranged to support progressive learning from basic to complex. By combining these characters, users can create over 380,000 Chinese words, making it a powerful tool for language mastery. The app includes calligraphy practice, allowing users to see characters in standard Chinese script. Tattoo artists can also explore various character designs for inspiration. To make learning fun, the app features puzzles and mystery games that reinforce memory and engagement. Daily recitation helps users retain knowledge and advance through levels from 1 to 52. A Speak button has been added to enhance listening skills and pronunciation practice. The app supports zoom, swipe, and flip gestures, making navigation intuitive and user-friendly. For those who prefer physical study materials, cards can be printed via AirPrint-compatible or Brother label printers. Traditional Chinese characters are fully supported, ensuring an authentic learning experience.
