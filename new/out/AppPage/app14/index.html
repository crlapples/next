PartyPhoto

PartyPhoto offers zero setup with 150 free templates for lifetime use with a one-time fee. It’s perfect for all events and works on both iPhone and iPad. Unlike other apps, PartyPhoto starts instantly, with no long setup required. It provides 150 high-resolution templates, free for unlimited use, and supports a range of photo configurations from 1 to 7 photos. You can print in various shapes, such as circles, squares, hearts, and more. It supports printing to a wide range of printers, including portable options like the Brother VC-500W. PartyPhoto also features smart template customization, allowing you to add your branding to any template. You can preview, save, print, or share photos, and customize settings like camera, flash, and countdown. With templates for various themes such as birthdays, weddings, and parties, PartyPhoto is a versatile tool for any event. It’s designed to be user-friendly, with no learning curve, making it perfect for any occasion.
