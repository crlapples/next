Brother iPrint

The Brother iPrint app allows you to print from any app that supports Print or Share features with Brother printers (TD, QL, MW, RJ, PT, PJ) via WiFi, Bluetooth, Direct WiFi, and MFi. It's perfect for both home and office use, offering quick and easy setup. Once installed, the app remembers all your settings and defaults to the most common ones, saving you time. Even if no Brother printer is connected, the app still lets you view compatible printers' settings. The initial setup process takes less than 10 seconds, and printing is as simple as tapping once. Unlike AirPrint, this app offers advanced printing options like setting halftone, media selection, auto cut, and orientation. It also supports faster printing and works with Bluetooth and MFi, unlike AirPrint, which only supports WiFi. The app automatically appears in the pop-up menu when you want to print, requiring no manual opening. Brother iPrint is compatible with a variety of Brother printers, including the QL, RJ, TD, and PT series. This app offers flexibility and control, making it a great choice for users with multiple Brother devices.
